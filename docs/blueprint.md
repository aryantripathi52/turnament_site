# **App Name**: Free Fire Frenzy

## Core Features:

- Tournament Listings: Display a list of upcoming Free Fire tournaments with relevant details such as date, time, prize pool, and region.
- Tournament Details: Provide a dedicated page for each tournament with in-depth information including rules, registration process, and contact details.
- Registration Form: Implement a simple form for users to register for tournaments.  Collect necessary information like team name, contact details, and player IDs.
- Announcements: Show announcements regarding the platform or related Free Fire eSport events. 
- AI-powered match highlight tool: The tool detects exciting plays and key moments in streamed Free Fire matches to automatically generate highlight reels that would be visible on the main screen. Highlights emphasize engagements involving players with high viewer numbers. The algorithm incorporates various stats such as kill count, in addition to information from shoutcasters.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Intense orange (#FF8C00) to convey energy and excitement of eSports.
- Background color: Dark grey (#222222) to provide contrast and focus on the content.
- Accent color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF) to highlight interactive elements and call to action buttons.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, for a modern and technical feel. Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif.
- Use clean and sharp icons relevant to eSports and Free Fire, such as trophies, players, and game elements.
- Dynamic and responsive layout to ensure readability and usability across different devices.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user experience and create a visually appealing interface.
